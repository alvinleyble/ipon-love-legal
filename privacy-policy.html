Privacy Policy — Love, Ipon

Last updated: July 4, 2026

Love, Ipon ("the app") is a couples personal finance and notes app developed by Alvin. This policy explains what data the app collects, how it's used, and how it's protected.

Data we collect

  • Account info: email address, display name, and a user ID, created when you register.
  • Financial data: transactions, accounts, categories, budgets, and recurring rules you enter into the app.
  • Notes: note text and any images you attach.
  • Couple pairing info: if you pair with a partner, the link between your account and theirs, used to show shared budgets and any content you choose to share.

We do not collect your location, contacts, calendar, SMS, health data, or browsing history. The app does not use analytics, advertising, or crash-reporting SDKs.

How we use this data

All data collected is used solely to provide the app's core functionality: authenticating your account, syncing your data across your devices, and enabling the couple-sharing features you choose to use. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to operating the app.

Where your data is stored

Data is stored using Supabase (Postgres database and file storage), which acts as our infrastructure provider. All data is encrypted in transit (TLS/HTTPS). Supabase does not use your data for its own purposes — it processes data solely on our behalf to operate the app.

Sharing with your partner

If you pair with a partner in the app, certain data (such as a shared budget or notes you explicitly mark as shared) becomes visible to them. Your individual account balance and unshared data remain private. You can unpair at any time, which stops any further sharing.

Third-party sharing

We do not sell or share your data with third parties. We do not use advertising or analytics services.

Data retention and deletion

Deleted records are retained in a "soft-deleted" state to keep sync working correctly across your own devices, but are no longer visible in the app. You can request full deletion of your account and all associated data at any time — see our account deletion page for instructions. Requests are processed within 30 days.

Security

All data in transit between the app and our servers is encrypted using TLS. Access to stored data is restricted to what's needed to operate the app.

Children's privacy

Love, Ipon is not directed at children and is intended for users 18 and over.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the "Last updated" date above will be revised. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
