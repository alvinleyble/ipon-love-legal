Delete Your Account — Love, Ipon

Love, Ipon is a couples personal finance and notes app developed by Alvin. This page explains how to delete your account and associated data.

Option 1: Delete instantly in the app (recommended)

If you still have the app installed, this is the fastest way — deletion happens immediately, with no waiting period.

Go to Settings → Profile → Account → Delete account, confirm with your password, and your account and all associated data are permanently deleted right away.

Option 2: Request deletion by email

If you no longer have the app installed or can't sign in, send an email to alvinpride.ani@gmail.com from the email address associated with your Love, Ipon account, with the subject line Delete my account. This request will be fulfilled manually within 30 days of verification.

What gets deleted

Whether deleted in-app or by request, the following is permanently deleted:

  • Your account credentials (email, authentication data)
  • Transactions, accounts, categories, budgets, and recurring rules
  • Notes and any attached images
  • Couple pairing information linking you to a partner account
Note on shared data: If you are paired with a partner, any content you explicitly shared with them (e.g. shared notes) may remain visible to your partner until they also delete it or unpair, consistent with how the sharing feature works while your account is active.

Data retention

No data is retained after deletion beyond what may be legally required (e.g. financial records for fraud prevention or legal compliance, if applicable), and any such data is not used for any other purpose.
